Our Research

Dr. Ye Tian Laboratory is located in the Division of Thoracic Surgery, the Fourth Affiliated Hospital of China Medical University. Our research focus lies at the clinical and basic research on lung cancer and other thoracic tumors.

Current subjects of study include microinvasive therapy and ERAS in thoracic surgery, comprehensive research and therapy in lung cancers, as well as physical training before/after thoracic surgery.
